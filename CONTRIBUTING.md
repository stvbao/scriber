# Contributing

Scriber is currently distributed under `GPL-3.0-only`.

By intentionally submitting code, documentation, assets, or other material
for inclusion in this repository, you represent that you have the right to
submit it and agree that your contribution may be distributed under
`GPL-3.0-only`.

To keep future licensing options open for the project, you also agree that
the maintainers may relicense your contribution as part of future versions of
Scriber.

If you are not comfortable with that, open an issue before sending a pull
request.
